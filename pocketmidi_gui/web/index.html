CONTROLS
INTENSITY
How much humanisation. 0.2–0.5 is the sweet spot; 1.0 is raw drummer spread. Click a lane name on the screen to set it for just that drum (a dot marks lanes with their own setting; KIT or Esc returns to the whole kit).
TIGHTNESS
How much the kit moves as one pocket vs every hit drifting on its own. Kit-wide — the whole kit shares one internal clock, so a lane can be tighter, but not on a separate clock.
LEAN
Kit-wide. Right (push) re-applies the source drummers' own per-drum lean habits; left is the mirror image of them — it inverts those tendencies, it doesn't add a flat delay. Centre = neutral, on the grid.
ALL CH
Humanise drum notes on every MIDI channel, not just channel 10.
HUMANISE
Apply the groove — every press is a fresh take with your current settings. UNDO swaps back to the previous render (again = redo).
TRANSPORT
⏮ top · « » step a bar (← →) · drag the ruler or click the screen to move the playhead.
ORIGINAL / HUMANISED
What you're hearing — blue is the original, amber is humanised. Click (or press A) to flip, even mid-playback.
ENGINE
Lit when the drummer profile is loaded and ready.
SCREEN
Click = set play position · drag = pan · ⌘wheel = zoom · double-click = fit · ghosts = the other version's positions.
